## Decisions Made

**1. Use warning log (not error) for unreachable service**
- Rationale: Service being temporarily unreachable is expected/transient, not an error condition
- Implementation: `logger.warning()` for unreachable service, `logger.error()` for missing config

**2. Skip job submission entirely when service unreachable**
- Rationale: Prevents creating failed job records that pollute job history
- Implementation: Return early before `submit_job()` call - no job record created

**3. Catch broad Exception for decrypt/validate operations**
- Rationale: Scheduler must not crash on individual list import failures
- Implementation: Try/except around decrypt_data and validate_api_key with error logging

**4. Health check placement between is_active and overlap checks**
- Rationale: After basic list validation, before expensive overlap detection
- Implementation: Inserted after is_active check (line ~199), before is_list_running check (line ~201)
